Cobblemon Customizables skin folder

Put Cobblemon skin packs here as .zip files or folders.

A valid pack usually contains:
- pack.mcmeta
- data/cobblemon/species_features/
- data/cobblemon/species_feature_assignments/
- data/cobblemon/species_additions/
- assets/cobblemon/bedrock/pokemon/resolvers/
- assets/cobblemon/textures/pokemon/

The mod detects packs here and registers them as required Minecraft packs on startup.
After adding or changing packs while the game is open, reload resources or restart the game.
